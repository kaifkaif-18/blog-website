Shah Rukh Khan Meets MI Players Ahead Of WPL Season Opener; Teaches Youngster ‘Bade Bade Desho Mein..’ Dialogue

Shah Rukh Khan teaches youngster 'bade bade desho mein.. : dialogue

By Omair Pall Feb. 23, 2024

As the Women's Premier League (WPL) opening ceremony draws near, Shah Rukh Khan, Bollywood's beloved superstar, isn't just gearing up to dazzle audiences with his performance; he's also spreading joy among the players of Delhi Capitals (DC) and Mumbai Indians (MI). Before the grand event kicked off at the M Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru, Shah Rukh Khan shared some heartfelt moments with cricketing stars, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

One of the most memorable instances captured on social media was Shah Rukh Khan's iconic arm spread pose alongside former Australian cricketer and current DC captain, Meg Lanning. Their camaraderie and charm were palpable, reflecting the seamless blend of Bollywood glamour and cricketing prowess.

But Shah Rukh Khan's interactions didn't stop there. As he made his way through the MI camp, his infectious energy spread like wildfire, as he shared hugs, greetings, and even a touch of Bollywood magic with Issy Wong. He took a moment to teach her a famous line from the cult classic, Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge, leaving everyone in awe of his warmth and star power.

In a heartwarming reunion, Shah Rukh Khan embraced his old friend, Sourav Ganguly. The video of their heartfelt hug reignited memories of their time together as captain and owner of Kolkata Knight Riders during the early days of the IPL. Despite their different paths since then, the bond between these two legends remains unbreakable..

As the excitement for the WPL's second season reaches a fever pitch, Shah Rukh Khan, along with Shahid Kapoor, Kartik Aryan, Tiger Shroff, and Varun Dhawan, is all set to light up the stage at the opening ceremony. The eve of the event witnessed Shah Rukh Khan rehearsing his signature hook step, 'Jhoome Jo Pathaan,' on stage, setting the tone for an unforgettable evening of entertainment and celebration.
